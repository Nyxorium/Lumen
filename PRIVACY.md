# Privacy Policy

Pronouns for Bluesky does not collect or transmit any data to third parties.

When you sign in, your handle and app password are used to authenticate 
with your account's PDS (Personal Data Server) and the AT Protocol. These credentials are 
stored locally in your browser using extension storage and are only 
transmitted to your account's PDS in accordance with the AT Protocol.

Your pronouns are saved directly to your account's repo when you click Save.

No analytics, tracking, or data collection of any kind takes place.
